Rules

  • - There are total 3 rounds. If score ties after 3 rounds, then an additional round is added until winner is declared.

    - A player has five items: Rock, Paper, Scissor, Lizard, Spock.

    - The game includes Lizard and Spock so there are fewer ties when you play (inspired from a TV Show - 'The Big Bang Theory').

    - A player can choose one of the items.

    - Scissor cuts Paper. Scissor wins.

    - Paper covers Rock. Paper wins.

    - Rock crushes Lizard. Rock wins.

    - Lizard poisons Spock. Lizard wins.

    - Spock smashes Scissor. Spock wins.

    - Scissor decapitates Lizard . Scissor wins.

    - Lizard eats Paper. Lizard wins.

    - Paper disproves Spock. Paper wins.

    - Spock vaporizes Rock. Spock wins.

    - Rock crushes Scissor Rock wins.
